Exercise UI (EUI) - Student Mode

The EUI allows you, as the student, to perform the exercise and afterwards be assessed against the instructor's solution.

  1. The EUI is launched by selecting an exercise URL. Click the 'Select Exercise' button below the image.
  2. Click the Reset button once after the scene loads to refresh the assessment engine.
  3. Load objects into the scene by selecting available assets (e.g. M4 Carbine) in the EUI upper-right corner.
  4. Click on the relevant areas of the object to initiate actions. A context menu shows the available actions.
  5. Perform the exercise steps in the proper sequence.
  6. When complete, select the Assessment button in the EUI upper-left corner to receive feedback.

Result: the assessment is displayed in a window within the EUI, summarizing any errors that made.
